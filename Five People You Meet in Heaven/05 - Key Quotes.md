# 05 - Key Quotes

Curated quotes from the novel, organized by theme and speaker. Use these for study, reflection, or discussion.

## On Life and Death

> [!quote] Opening Line
> *"This is a story about a man named Eddie and it begins at the end, with Eddie dying in the sun. It might seem strange to start a story with an ending. But all endings are also beginnings. We just don't know it at the time."*

> [!quote] The Nature of Heaven
> *"This is the greatest gift God can give you: to understand what happened in your life. To have it explained. It is the peace you have been searching for."*
> — [[The Blue Man]]

> [!quote] Time in Heaven
> *"Dying? Not the end of everything. We think it is. But what happens on earth is only the beginning."*
> — [[The Captain]]

## Lesson 1: Connection

> [!quote] The Blue Man on Connection
> *"There are no random acts. That we are all connected. That you can no more separate one life from another than you can separate a breeze from the wind."*

> [!quote] Fairness
> *"Fairness does not govern life and death. If it did, no good person would ever die young."*

> [!quote] No Life Is a Waste
> *"No life is a waste. The only time we waste is the time we spend thinking we are alone."*

> [!quote] Strangers
> *"Strangers are just family you have yet to come to know."*

## Lesson 2: Sacrifice

> [!quote] The Captain on Sacrifice
> *"Sacrifice is a part of life. It's supposed to be. It's not something to regret. It's something to aspire to."*

> [!quote] Passing It On
> *"Sometimes when you sacrifice something precious, you're not really losing it. You're just passing it on to someone else."*

> [!quote] The Promise
> *"I got to keep my promise. I didn't leave you behind."*

> [!quote] The Shot
> *"I took your leg to save your life."*

## Lesson 3: Forgiveness

> [!quote] Ruby on Anger
> *"Holding anger is a poison. It eats you from inside. We think that hating is a weapon that attacks the person who harmed us. But hatred is a curved blade. And the harm we do, we do to ourselves."*

> [!quote] Forgive
> *"Forgive, Edward. Forgive."*

> [!quote] It's Fixed
> *"It's fixed."* — Eddie's final words to his father

> [!quote] Loyalty
> *"Better to be loyal to one another."*

## Lesson 4: Love

> [!quote] Marguerite on Lost Love
> *"Lost love is still love, Eddie. It takes a different form, that's all... Life has to end. Love doesn't."*

> [!quote] Memory
> *"Memory becomes your partner. You nurture it. You hold it. You dance with it."*

> [!quote] You Loved Me Anyway
> *"No, you didn't. I was right here. And you loved me anyway."*

> [!quote] The Bingo Cards
> *"All that's missing is the bingo cards."*

## Lesson 5: Purpose

> [!quote] Tala on Purpose
> *"Children. You keep them safe. You make good for me. Is where you were supposed to be, Eddie Main-ten-ance."*

> [!quote] Tala's Hands
> *"Not her hands. My hands. I bring you to heaven. Keep you safe."*

> [!quote] Supposed to Be There
> *"Supposed to be there."* — pointing to Ruby Pier

## On Stories

> [!quote] Stories Intersect
> *"No story sits by itself. Sometimes stories meet at corners and sometimes they cover one another completely, like stones beneath a river."*

> [!quote] The Epilogue
> *"The world is full of stories, but the stories are all one."*
