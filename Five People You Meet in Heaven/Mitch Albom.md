# Mitch Albom

## About the Author

Mitch Albom is an American author, journalist, screenwriter, dramatist, radio and television broadcaster, and musician. He is best known for the inspirational themes in his writing.

## Major Works

- **Tuesdays with Morrie** (1997) — Memoir about his former professor
- **The Five People You Meet in Heaven** (2003) — This novel
- **For One More Day** (2006)
- **The Time Keeper** (2012)
- **The First Phone Call from Heaven** (2013)

## Connection to This Book

Albom dedicated *The Five People You Meet in Heaven* to his uncle, **Edward Beitchman**, who gave him the concept of heaven. Every Thanksgiving, his uncle spoke of a night in the hospital when he awoke to see the souls of his departed loved ones sitting on the edge of his bed, waiting for him.

> *"Everyone has an idea of heaven, as do most religions, and they should all be respected. The version represented here is only a guess, a wish, in some ways, that my uncle, and others like him — people who felt unimportant here on earth — realize, finally, how much they mattered and how they were loved."*
